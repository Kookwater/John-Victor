My Story

My name is John Victor, I am 53 years old, and I live in Swakopmund, Namibia. For most of my life, I have been an outdoor person who loved nature and independence.

In January 2021, everything changed. I was diagnosed with Multiple Sclerosis (MS) by a neurologist in Windhoek. This disease has left me wheelchair‑bound, unable to work, and dependent on a dear friend for daily care.

This illness has taken away my independence—but it has not taken away my hope.

I have learned of a stem-cell treatment that could help repair the damage in my body. For me, this treatment is more than medicine—it is a chance to walk again, support myself, and live with dignity.

I have never asked for financial help before. This is my first and only cry for support.
